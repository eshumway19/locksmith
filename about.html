About All Secure Lock

Family-owned and operated since 1994 — bringing integrity, expertise, and a genuine commitment to your security.

Our Story

Dan and Shannon Pointer built All Secure Lock on a simple idea: treat every customer's home or business like it's your own. Dan got his start in locksmithing under the guidance of Dwight Stone at Stone's Lock and Key, and he's been sharpening that craft ever since through decades of residential and commercial work across Utah.

After years serving communities in southern Utah, the Pointers relocated to Cache Valley, where they've quickly become the area's go-to resource for everything from emergency lockouts to full commercial access control systems. They brought their tools, their experience, and a no-pressure approach that Cache Valley customers have responded to warmly.

Today, All Secure Lock is one of the few local providers with deep expertise in both traditional mechanical locksmithing and modern electronic security — giving you a single, trusted resource for any lock or security need.

Service Area

We proudly serve over 50 cities across Cache, Weber, Box Elder, and Rich Counties in Utah, as well as Franklin County in Idaho. Whether you're in Logan, Ogden, Smithfield, or a smaller community in our region, we're here for you.

Hours & Availability

Regular service is by appointment. Emergency calls are answered daily — including weekends and holidays — from 5–8 AM and again from 4–11 PM. If it's outside those windows, leave a message and Dan will get back to you as soon as possible.
