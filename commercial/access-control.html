Access Control Systems

Modern, scalable access control for businesses of all sizes — from a single door to enterprise-wide systems.

Take Control of Who Enters Your Business

A key can be copied, lost, or handed to the wrong person — and you'd never know. Access control gives you a completely different level of oversight: you decide who gets in, which doors they can open, and what hours they're allowed. All Secure Lock designs, installs, and maintains access control systems for businesses of all sizes across northern Utah and southern Idaho.

Types of Access Control We Install

  • ✓ Card & Key Fob Readers — Proximity and smart card systems for fast, contactless entry
  • ✓ Keypad / PIN Systems — Code-based access without needing to issue physical credentials
  • ✓ Mobile Credentials — Use smartphones (Apple Wallet, Google Wallet) as access credentials
  • ✓ Video Intercom / Buzzer — See and speak with visitors before granting access
  • ✓ Cloud-Based Management — Manage access remotely, view audit logs, add/revoke credentials instantly

Key Benefits

  • ✓ Instantly revoke access when an employee leaves — no rekeying required
  • ✓ Set time-based schedules (e.g., cleaners can enter Mon–Fri 7–9 PM only)
  • ✓ Detailed audit trails showing who entered where and when
  • ✓ Integrate with existing alarm systems, cameras, and building management
  • ✓ Scale from one door to hundreds without replacing hardware
  • ✓ Real-time alerts for unauthorized access attempts

Industries We Serve

We've installed access control for medical clinics, schools, offices, warehouses, retail stores, apartment buildings, and government facilities. Every project starts with understanding how your people move through your building — then we design a system around that, not the other way around.

We start with a free on-site visit to evaluate your doors, identify the right credential type, and give you a realistic quote. From there we handle wiring, hardware, programming, and walk your staff through how to use the system. No subcontractors — just us, start to finish.
