ADA Compliance

The ADA sets specific requirements: no more than 5 lbs of force to open interior doors, activator buttons mounted between 15–48" from the floor, and doors that hold open long enough for someone using a wheelchair or walker to pass through safely. We make sure every installation meets those standards — not just on paper, but in practice.
