Where to Place Cameras

For homes, we focus on exterior coverage — front door, garage, side gates, and rear access points. Interior cameras in living areas raise privacy concerns we'd rather help you avoid.

For commercial properties, we do a full walk of your site to map entry points, parking, loading areas, server rooms, and any high-value storage. The goal is complete coverage with no blind spots and no wasted cameras.
