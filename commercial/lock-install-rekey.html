The Foundation of Physical Security

Electronic access control handles a lot — but most commercial buildings still rely on mechanical locks for plenty of doors. Getting those locks right matters. All Secure Lock installs, rekeyes, and maintains commercial-grade hardware for businesses throughout northern Utah and southern Idaho, including high-security locks for areas that need an extra layer of protection.

  • ✓ High-security locks (Medeco, Mul-T-Lock, ABLOY)
  • ✓ Mortise locks for commercial doors
  • ✓ Cylindrical and tubular locks
  • ✓ Padlocks and hasps for gates and storage
  • ✓ Restricted keyway systems (key control)

Master Key Systems

One key for management, different keys for staff, another for cleaning crews — a master key system makes that possible without a complicated electronic system. We design and install custom hierarchies for apartment complexes, office buildings, hotels, schools, and any multi-tenant or multi-department facility that needs organized access.

  • ✓ Grand master, master, and change key levels
  • ✓ Cross-keying for shared access
  • ✓ Expandable systems that grow with your facility
  • ✓ Key control systems with restricted duplication

Commercial Rekeying

Employee turnover, lost keys, a security incident, taking over a new space — any of these is a good reason to rekey. We can handle a single door or your entire facility in one visit, and we'll issue new keys on the spot.
