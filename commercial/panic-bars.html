Panic Hardware: A Life-Safety Priority

In a real emergency, people push — they don't search for a handle. Panic bars are code-required on most commercial exits because they let anyone get out fast, while still keeping unauthorized people from getting in. All Secure Lock installs, repairs, and services panic hardware throughout northern Utah and southern Idaho, and we make sure everything meets current life-safety codes.

Door Closer Services

A door that doesn't fully close is a door that isn't secure — and in a fire-rated corridor, it's a code violation. We install, adjust, and replace door closers on all types of commercial doors.

  • ✓ Surface-mounted door closers
  • ✓ Concealed door closers
  • ✓ ADA-compliant low-energy closers
  • ✓ Adjustment and repair of existing closers

Should You Repair or Replace?

Many door closer issues — slamming, slow closing, not latching — can be resolved with a simple adjustment or minor repair. If the unit is leaking hydraulic fluid or structurally damaged, replacement is usually more cost-effective. We'll give you an honest assessment.
