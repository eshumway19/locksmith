Commercial Safes & Vaults

Protect your business assets — cash, documents, data, and valuables — with professionally installed commercial-grade safes.

Serious Protection for Serious Assets

Locks and cameras protect your perimeter. A safe protects what's inside. Whether you're securing end-of-day cash, confidential records, firearms, or irreplaceable documents, All Secure Lock will help you find the right safe for the job and install it properly — including anchoring, which most people skip and almost always regret.

Commercial Safe Types

  • ✓ Burglary-resistant safes — Rated by TL (Tool-Resistant), TR (Torch-Resistant), or TRTL ratings
  • ✓ Fire-rated safes — UL-rated for document, media, or data protection at specified temperatures
  • ✓ Combination safes — Fire AND burglary protection in a single unit
  • ✓ Depository safes — Drop slots for cash deposits without opening the main compartment
  • ✓ Floor safes — Concealed in-floor installation for maximum security
  • ✓ Wall safes — Space-saving concealed installation
  • ✓ Vault doors — Convert a room into a walk-in vault

Lock Types Available

  • ✓ Electronic keypad locks
  • ✓ Traditional combination dial locks
  • ✓ Biometric (fingerprint) locks
  • ✓ Dual-control locks (requires two people)
  • ✓ Time-lock mechanisms

Professional Anchoring

An unanchored safe — even a heavy one — can be tipped, rolled out on a dolly, and cracked open off-site. We bolt every safe to the floor or wall as part of the installation. It takes 20 minutes and makes an enormous difference.
