Modern Credential Technology for Your Business

The card reader is what your employees, tenants, and visitors interact with every day. Picking the right technology — and installing it correctly — determines how smooth that experience is and how secure the system actually is. All Secure Lock installs and services every major card reader type for businesses running anywhere from a single door to a multi-site system.

Card Reader Technologies

  • ✓ 125kHz Proximity (Prox) Cards — Classic, low-cost contactless card technology
  • ✓ 13.56MHz Smart Cards (MIFARE, DESFire) — Higher security with encrypted communication
  • ✓ Mobile Credentials (BLE) — Use smartphones or Apple Watch as access credentials
  • ✓ Apple Wallet / Google Wallet — Digital keys stored in native mobile wallets
  • ✓ Multi-Technology Readers — Support multiple credential types on one reader
  • ✓ Keypad + Card (Multi-Factor) — Require both card and PIN for high-security areas

Installation Options

  • ✓ Indoor and outdoor weatherproof readers
  • ✓ Mullion-mount (door frame) and gang box mount
  • ✓ Wiegand, OSDP, and RS-485 panel connections
  • ✓ IP/network-connected readers for cloud management

Credential Management

Modern systems allow you to add, modify, and revoke credentials instantly — from anywhere. Lost a card? Disable it immediately in software without ever replacing hardware. Employee left? Revoke access in seconds, not days.
