Rekeying vs. Replacing

Rekeying changes the internal pins of your existing lock so old keys no longer work. The hardware stays in place — it's faster and significantly cheaper than replacement. Rekeying is the right call when:

  • ✓ You just moved into a new home and don't know who has keys
  • ✓ A key was lost or not returned
  • ✓ You've had a breakup or ended a tenant relationship
  • ✓ You want all your locks on one key

Lock replacement is recommended when your lock is old, worn, damaged, or when you want to upgrade to a higher security rating. We'll give you an honest recommendation.
