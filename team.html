Dan Pointer

Co-Owner & Master Locksmith

Dan's love for locksmithing began in childhood. He trained under the guidance of Dwight Stone at Stone's Lock and Key right here in Logan, Utah — building a foundation of technical skill and professional ethics that has defined his career for over 30 years.

After honing his craft in Southern Utah, Dan relocated to Cache Valley with his wife Shannon, where they established All Secure Lock as a cornerstone of the community's security industry. Dan is proficient in both traditional mechanical locksmithing and modern electronic security systems including smart locks, access control, and surveillance technology.

Dan holds an active ALOA (Associated Locksmiths of America) membership and keeps current with the latest advancements in the security industry through continuing education and professional development.

When he's not working, Dan enjoys gardening, exploring the outdoors, and spending time with his family in the beautiful Cache Valley.

ALOA Certified 30+ Years Experience Electronic Security Specialist

Shannon Pointer

Co-Owner & Operations

Shannon Pointer is the operational backbone of All Secure Lock. Together with Dan, she has helped grow the business from a local locksmith service into a comprehensive security solutions company serving over 50 communities across two states.

Shannon manages customer relationships, scheduling, and business operations — ensuring every client receives prompt, professional service from the first phone call through job completion. Her warmth and dedication to customer satisfaction are a big reason why All Secure Lock has earned such a loyal following in Cache Valley.

Shannon shares Dan's love of the outdoors and community. Together, the Pointers are committed to giving back to the Cache Valley area that has supported their business for over three decades.
